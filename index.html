02 — Materi

Daily Activities & Routines

What Are Daily Activities?

Daily activities are the things we do every day as part of our routine — from waking up in the morning to going to sleep at night. In English, we talk about daily routines using the Simple Present Tense combined with time expressions and adverbs of frequency. Understanding how people in West Sulawesi organize their day gives us a rich, authentic context to practise this language.

A Fisherman's Day in Majene

West Sulawesi Coast — Mandar Fishing Community

Reading Text 1
Introduction

Meet Pak Ruslan, a fisherman from Majene, West Sulawesi. Like many in the Mandar community, his daily routine is shaped by the sea. His schedule is very different from a city dweller's, but it is full of purpose, community, and tradition.

His Daily Schedule
  • 04:00 Pak Ruslan wakes up before sunrise. He performs Subuh prayer with his family, then prepares his fishing equipment on the perahu sandeq.
  • 05:00 He sails out to sea with other fishermen. They cast their nets in designated fishing spots passed down through generations.
  • 09:00 He returns to shore with the day's catch. His wife sorts and sells the fish at the local market (pasar ikan). This is their main livelihood.
  • 12:00 The family gathers for lunch. They eat pallu butung — a traditional local dish — prepared fresh by his wife.
  • 14:00 Pak Ruslan repairs his fishing nets under the coconut trees. He teaches his son how to weave new nets — passing down a skill from his own father.
  • 18:00 After Maghrib prayer, the family sits together on the teras (porch). They share stories and listen to kalinda'da' poetry from the elders.
  • 21:00 The family goes to bed early to prepare for another predawn start the next morning. "The sea waits for no one," Pak Ruslan always says.

A Student's Daily Life in Mamuju

Mamuju City — Capital of West Sulawesi Province

Reading Text 2
Introduction

Sari is a 16-year-old student at SMA Negeri 1 Mamuju, the provincial capital of West Sulawesi. Her daily routine blends modern student life with the strong cultural values of her Mandar family.

Sari's Daily Schedule
  • 05:00Sari wakes up, prays Subuh, and helps her mother prepare breakfast. She usually eats nasi kuning or roti bakar before school.
  • 06:30She gets dressed in her school uniform and walks to school with her neighbour. She always arrives before the bell rings at 7:00.
  • 07:00School starts. Sari pays attention in class and takes notes carefully. She particularly enjoys her English and Biology lessons.
  • 12:00During the lunch break, Sari eats at the school canteen with her friends. They sometimes discuss their homework or plan upcoming school events.
  • 14:00School ends. Sari goes home, changes her clothes, and takes a short nap before the afternoon prayer.
  • 16:00She does her homework and sometimes joins an online study group with friends. She also practises speaking English by watching YouTube videos.
  • 19:30After dinner, the family watches local television together. Sari's grandmother often tells stories about Mandar traditions before bedtime.
💭 Discussion Question

Compare Pak Ruslan's schedule with Sari's. What are the similarities and differences? How does the sea influence daily life in West Sulawesi?

04 — Grammar Focus

Simple Present Tense & Adverbs of Frequency

Dua fitur kebahasaan utama dalam mendeskripsikan kegiatan harian: Simple Present Tense untuk menyatakan rutinitas, dan adverbs of frequency untuk menunjukkan seberapa sering.

A. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta, kebiasaan, dan rutinitas yang berlangsung secara reguler.

Formula
Subject (I/You/We/They) + Verb (base form) Subject (He/She/It) + Verb + -s / -es ✓ "I wake up at 5 AM every day." ✓ "Pak Ruslan sails out to sea before sunrise." ✗ WRONG: "Sari wake up at 5 AM." → CORRECT: "Sari wakes up at 5 AM."
SubjectVerb FormExample
I / You / We / TheyBase formgo → "They go to school at 7."
He / She / It+ swork → "She works at the market."
He / She / It+ es (after s,sh,ch,x,o)watch → "He watches TV at night."
He / She / Ity → iesstudy → "Sari studies every evening."

B. Adverbs of Frequency

Kata keterangan yang menunjukkan seberapa sering suatu kegiatan dilakukan. Posisinya biasanya sebelum verb utama (kecuali setelah "be").

always
100% — Selalu
usually
80% — Biasanya
often
60% — Sering
sometimes
40% — Kadang-kadang
rarely
20% — Jarang
never
0% — Tidak pernah
Position Rules
Subject + Adverb + Main Verb ✓ "Pak Ruslan always wakes up early." Subject + be + Adverb ✓ "Sari is usually on time for school."
⚠️ Common Mistake

Jangan letakkan adverb setelah verb utama: ✗ "She eats always rice for breakfast."
Yang benar: ✓ "She always eats rice for breakfast."

06 — Rangkuman

What We Have Learned

Ringkasan poin-poin kunci dari seluruh materi e-module ini.

📋 Daily Activities

  • Rutinitas dibagi 4 waktu: morning, afternoon, evening, night
  • Dipengaruhi profesi, budaya, dan nilai lokal
  • Kehidupan nelayan Mandar berpusat pada laut
  • Siswa modern memadukan tradisi dan teknologi

📐 Simple Present Tense

  • I/You/We/They + verb (base form)
  • He/She/It + verb + s/es/ies
  • Digunakan untuk rutinitas & fakta umum
  • Negative: do not / does not + base verb

🔁 Adverbs of Frequency

  • always → usually → often → sometimes → rarely → never
  • Posisi: sebelum verb utama
  • Setelah "be": She is always punctual
  • Bisa di awal kalimat: Sometimes she is late

📖 Key Vocabulary

  • routine, wake up, prepare, perform
  • livelihood, gather, commute, concentrate
  • productive, regularly, blend, tradition
